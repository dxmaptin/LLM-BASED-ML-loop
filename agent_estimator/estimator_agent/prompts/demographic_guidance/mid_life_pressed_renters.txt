Environmental & Social Attitudes:

- Ethical brand support is LOW-MODERATE - budget doesn't allow
- Environmental identity is LOW-MODERATE - focused on survival, not activism
- Recycling and environmental behaviors limited by rental housing situations
- Values exist but can't afford to act on them

Financial Behavior:
- HIGH saving intent but very difficult execution
- VERY HIGH price comparison (financial necessity)

KEY INSIGHT: This segment is defined by financial stress and housing insecurity. Despite potentially moderate values, their behavior is driven entirely by NECESSITY and CONSTRAINT.
